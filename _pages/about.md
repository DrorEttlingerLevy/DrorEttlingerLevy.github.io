## About Me

I am a PhD Researcher at the Faculty of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Technion – Israel Institute of Technology. My research focuses on marine monitoring using machine learning. 🌊

I am passionate about **computer vision**, **deep learning**, and **remote sensing**, and using these technologies to protect the environment. 🌱

### Education

- **M.Sc.** in **Predictive Hydrogeological Numerical Modeling** from the Weizmann Institute of Science.
- **B.Sc.** in **Soil and Water Sciences** from the Hebrew University.

### Research Interests

I'm particularly interested in the practical applications of **machine learning** and **deep learning** for environmental monitoring.  
Currently, I'm focusing on:
- **Computer vision** and **audio analysis** 
- **Generative models**
